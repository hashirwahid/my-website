What is Freelancing?

Freelancing means offering your skills online and getting paid per project. Students can earn money while studying by working remotely for clients worldwide.

Fiverr

Fiverr is a beginner-friendly freelancing platform where students can sell services starting from $5.

  • No experience required
  • Easy account creation
  • Clients come to you
  • Fast payments

Best for: Canva design, writing, video editing

Upwork

Upwork is a professional freelancing website with higher-paying clients and long-term projects.

  • Higher competition
  • Better income potential
  • Skill-based jobs

Best for: Developers, writers, marketers

Freelancer

Freelancer allows you to bid on projects posted by clients worldwide.

  • Wide range of jobs
  • Project-based work
  • International clients

Best for: Beginners & intermediate freelancers

PeoplePerHour

PeoplePerHour focuses on hourly and fixed-price freelance services.

  • Verified clients
  • Quality projects
  • Skill-focused platform

Best for: Designers & developers
